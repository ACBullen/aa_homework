Ingredients

  • 8-12 oz tortelini(fresh or dried)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 2 slices of bacon cut into small pieces
  • 1-2 shallots diced finely
  • 1/2 teaspoon of fresh thyme
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/8 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas

Baked Tortelini

Baked tortelini is a fairly modern spin on the classic dish of tortelini. The the tortelini is boiled as normal, but instead of just a classic sauce, such as a bechamel or light tomato sauce, we can build a kind of casserole around the pasta, with bacon and peas adding a little extra substance, and a crunchy topping made of bread crumbs and a bit of olive oil to add an extra textural layer to the dish.
